Avinab Saha

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Laboratory of Image and Video Engineering, The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Al Bovik. My ongoing research pursuits center around the realm of Perceptual Image & Video Quality Assessment, particularly concentrating on emerging technologies like Cloud Gaming & Virtual Reality applications.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at Samsung Research where I worked on developing AI-based low-complex real-time Image and Video Enhancement algorithms for Samsung Galaxy Mobile Phones and 8K Televisions. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Electrical Communications Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, India where I worked at the Visual Information Processing Lab with Prof. Jayanta Mukhopadhyay on accelerating Deep Learning models.
